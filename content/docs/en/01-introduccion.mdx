# Introduction & getting started

Mis Gastos is a **100% offline** personal finance app for Android. No account required, no data uploaded to any server, and it works without an internet connection.

What you can do with it:

- Record income, expenses, and transfers between accounts
- Manage multiple accounts in different currencies
- View visual reports of your finances
- Track debts and investments
- Back up all your data at any time

---

## Download and installation

Mis Gastos is distributed as a direct APK — it's not available in any app store.

<Steps>
  <Step number={1} title="Download the APK">
    Go to the [downloads page](/en/descargas) and tap **Download APK**. The file will be saved to your downloads folder.
  </Step>
  <Step number={2} title="Enable installation from unknown sources">
    Android blocks external APK installation by default. Go to **Settings > Security** (or **Settings > Apps > Install unknown apps**) and enable the permission for your browser or file manager.
  </Step>
  <Step number={3} title="Install the APK">
    Open the downloaded file and follow Android's installation prompts. The process takes a few seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step number={4} title="Open the app">
    Find **Mis Gastos** in your app drawer and open it. On the first launch you'll land directly on the home screen — no registration or login required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Installation permission">
  If Android shows "App blocked by Play Protect", tap **Install anyway**. The app contains no malicious code — the warning appears because it doesn't come from the Play Store.
</Callout>

## The home screen

When you open the app, the first thing you see is the **home dashboard**. It has three zones:

1. **Total net worth** — combined balance of all your accounts converted to the base currency.
2. **This month's income and expenses** — current month totals, also in the base currency.
3. **Recent transactions** — the last 8 records with type, account, and amount.

From the bottom bar you can navigate to the five main sections:

| Section | What it does |
|---|---|
| Home | Summary dashboard and recent transactions |
| Transactions | Full history with filters |
| Accounts | Account management and balances |
| Reports | Charts and analysis |
| More | Debts, investments, currencies, backup, settings |

## Recommended initial setup

Before recording your first transaction, it's worth doing three things:

### 1. Choose your base currency

The base currency is what the app uses to convert balances from different-currency accounts and display the total net worth. You can change it in **More > Settings**.

<Callout type="tip" title="Which base currency to choose?">
  Pick the currency you think in — usually the one of your country of residence. Once configured, all conversions go to that currency.
</Callout>

### 2. Add your currencies

If you operate with more than one currency (e.g., pesos and dollars), go to **More > Currencies** and add each one with its exchange rate. See [Currencies & exchange rates](/en/docs/05-monedas) for details.

### 3. Create your first accounts

Every wallet, card, or bank account is an **Account** in the app. Go to **Accounts > +** to add the first one. You can give it a name, assign a color, an icon, and the currency it operates in. See [Account management](/en/docs/02-cuentas) for the full process.

## How data flows

Mis Gastos has no backend. Everything lives in your device's local storage (Hive database). The flow is:

```
Your phone → Hive (local) → screen
```

Nothing leaves the device except when you choose to export a backup. This means:

- If you uninstall the app without a backup, **all data is lost**.
- If you switch phones, **you need to export and import** the JSON file.
- Two phones don't sync automatically — use the backup to transfer data.

<Callout type="info" title="Back up before uninstalling">
  Always export a backup from **More > Backup** before uninstalling the app or resetting your phone. The process takes less than five seconds.
</Callout>
